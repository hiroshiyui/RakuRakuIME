First public release (0.0.1).

* EZ (輕鬆輸入法) keyboard with radical composition, phrase rules,
  live candidate bar, and auto-refresh when the bundled CIN asset
  updates.
* Full PC-style English QWERTY with number row, brackets,
  punctuation, long-press shift-symbol alternates, Pro Shift key
  (one-shot / caps lock), and Google-10K word prediction.
* Emoji panel with eight categorised tabs (1,184 emojis).
* Dialer-style number pad.
* Material You dynamic theming on Android 12+, with a built-in
  Solarized fallback selectable in Settings.
* Roboto Slab typeface for alphanumerical keycaps.
* Per-app language picker, split landscape, adjustable keyboard
  height, optional keypress vibration, full TalkBack semantics.
* Fully offline — no network, no analytics, cloud auto-backup
  disabled.
