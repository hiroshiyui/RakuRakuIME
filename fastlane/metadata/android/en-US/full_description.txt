RakuRaku IME is a modern rebuild of the classic EZ Input Method (輕鬆輸入法)
for Android, built with Jetpack Compose and Room. Type Traditional Chinese
using 高衡緒's shape-based radical system, with a live candidate bar and
near-instant prefix matching powered by a full-text-search dictionary that
ships pre-packaged inside the app — no initial download required.

Features

* Full EZ radical layout with phrase composing rules.
* English word prediction backed by the Google 10K common-word corpus.
* Emoji keyboard with eight categories and 1,184 emojis.
* Dialer-style number pad.
* Split keyboard in landscape, adjustable height, optional keypress vibration.
* Material You theming on Android 12+, with a Solarized fallback on older
  devices or by user choice.
* Traditional Chinese (臺灣) and English UI; per-app language picker on
  Android 13+.
* Fully offline — no network access, no analytics, no tracking. Your
  keystrokes never leave the device.
