# Feature graphic

Google Play requires a **1024 × 500 PNG or JPEG** feature graphic.
F-Droid optionally renders one at the same size.

Drop the final artwork here as `featureGraphic.png` (lower-case `G`
is the filename both tools look for).

Tips:
* Avoid small text — the graphic shrinks in the carousel.
* Don't put essential content in the corners or edges — Play crops
  for different layouts.
* Google Play rejects images with the word "Google" or non-official
  Android robots. Use your own art.
